About

Quick Credit is a money lending app that provides short term soft loans to its clients. Clients register and apply for loans after their details have been verified. They can then repay those loans in installments within an agreed period of time

Getting Started

Here's how to get started

  1. Create a Client Account
  2. Wait for your details to be verified by the Admin
  3. Apply for a loan
  4. Make monthly repayments of the agreed installment amount
